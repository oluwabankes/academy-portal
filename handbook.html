Academic Integrity

Plagiarism, cheating, and any form of academic dishonesty are strictly prohibited and will result in disciplinary action, up to and including expulsion.

Dress Code

Students must adhere to the smart-casual dress code at all times on campus, including classrooms, cafeteria, and administrative areas.

Code of Conduct

Respect for faculty, staff, and fellow students is mandatory. Any breach of public peace or property will lead to sanctions.
